About me

Born and raised in Costa Rica, I have lived for the last 8 years in different parts of the world. In 2019 I moved to Maui, Hawaii, with my husband, and that is when I began to learn and explore my passion for photography and capturing the natural beauty that Maui had to offer, flourishing my craft that became my full-time job Through my lens, I have developed a sense of style of portrait themes, using natural scenery and golden light, that allows me to capture intimate connections and genuine smiles that result in unforgettable memories. I currently live in the Bahamas, the island is a showcase of natural beauty that makes it a photographers perfect backdrop for capturing those special moments. Living here thus far has been an incredible experience, learning the history, culture and enjoying the local festivities allows me to embrace and immerse myself in a vibrant community.
